Hampton Inn by Hilton Brampton - Toronto

Just 20 minutes’ drive from Toronto Pearson International Airport, this Brampton hotel features an indoor pool and includes a daily hot breakfast. All rooms include a microwave and fridge.

A 40-inch HD TV and free in-room movie channels are standard in all rooms at Hampton Inn by Hilton. Tea and coffee-making facilities are provided for added convenience, in addition to ironing facilities.

A hot and cold breakfast includes items such as waffles, oatmeal, juice and coffee. Breakfast bags are offered to guests who are on-the-go. Free tea and coffee is available throughout the day.

We speak your language!

Fairfield Inn & Suites by Marriott Toronto Brampton

Located in Brampton, this property provides daily breakfast and free WiFi. A fridge and a microwave are offered in each room. Downtown Toronto is 40 km away.

Each guest room includes a flat-screen cable TV. A seating area and a work desk are also included. Free toiletries are provided.

An indoor pool with water slide is featured at Brampton Fairfield Inn. Guests can relax in the hot tub or use the fitness centre. Fax, photocopying and laundry facilities are available.
